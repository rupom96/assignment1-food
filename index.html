My Recipies

When you need to feed the family or kids, these are the easy dinner recipes you turn to time and time again. Here are some recipies:


Chocolate Shake

In a blender, place milk and syrup. Cover and blend on high speed for 2 seconds. Add ice cream. Cover and blend on low speed about 5 seconds or until smooth. Pour into glasses. Serve immediately. Don’t forget a creamy topping, colorful sprinkles, or maraschino cherries. Look for refrigerated topping.

Beaf Steak

Place the beef on the grill rack over medium heat. Cover the grill; cook 6 to 8 minutes for rib eye, turning beef once halfway through cooking, until an instant-read meat thermometer inserted in center of thickest part reads 145°F for medium-rare or 160°F for medium doneness.

Thai Soup

Mix the chicken breast with ginger & garlic paste. Fry them in a pan with 1 tsp of vegetable oil for 5 mins. Then put them in a separate bowl. Take one heavy saucepan and pour 5 cups of cold water. Mix the corn flour with water. Then pour all the ingredients one by one.
